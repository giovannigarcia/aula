ATIVIDADE DE BIBLIOTECAS_-_API SIMPLE CARD MANAGER

OVERVIEW

The Simple Card Manager API offers the possibility to implement
a simple management of a deck cards, compound of 52 cards 
without jokers. The interface offers the possibilities to
shuffle the cards, to cut the stack, to get the top and bottom cards
and to query the discard stack. The initial functions are programmed,
by the way, in portuguese.
